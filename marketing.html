Marketing Ideas

  • Target audience: The marketing campaign will target families with children, as well as tech-savvy individuals who want the latest in home entertainment technology.
  • Promote the Al features: The Al capabilities of the TV, such as recognizing who is watching and suggesting appropriate content, will be highlighted in the marketing campaign.
  • Emphasize ease of use: The TV's automatic shut off and motorized tilting mechanism will be promoted as features that make it easy and convenient to use.
  • Offer a limited-time discount code to new customers who sign up for our email list
  • Create a referral program to encourage current customers to refer their friends and family
  • Offer installation and setup services: Offering installation and setup services to customers can help promote the TV as a hassle-free addition to their home entertainment system
  • Collaborate with complementary businesses to cross-promote our product

Business Strategies

  • Premium pricing: The TV's advanced Al features and high-end specs can justify a premium price point compared to other smart TVs in the market.
  • Licensing agreements: The Al technology used in the TV can be licensed to other manufacturers, generating additional revenue streams.
  • Data collection: The facial recognition and viewing habits data collected by the TV can be used to inform targeted advertising and content recommendations, generating additional revenue streams.
  • OUp-selling and cross-selling: The TV can be bundled with other smart home devices like voice assistants and home security systems to generate additional revenue through up-selling and cross-selling.
  • Strategic partnerships: Partnering with technology companies and content providers can help expand the reach of the TV and its Al capabilities
